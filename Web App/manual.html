This is a simple card game that lets you pick cards from a shuffled deck that is between 1 to 100. You will be picking 5 cards.

If the total added values divided by your age return an even integer, you gain 1 win point. But if it returns an odd number, you gain 1 fail point. If you score 5 win points, you won the game, but if you score 5 fail points, you lost the game. After that the program has a button for you to choose if you want to play another round.
